Unifide is a collaborative web platform created by a team of thinkers, misfits, and tinkerers with a shared passion for new technologies. Our goal is to disrupt current internet communication methods and fundamentally redefine how people use web applications and services. We aim to accomplish this by making internet services simpler and more interactive. Unifide uses modular web components to create frictionless workflows and media-rich experiences online. We are dedicated to the idea of bringing people together in new ways. We believe that mutual interests are just as important as mutual friends.
